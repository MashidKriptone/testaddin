KntrolEMAIL

KntrolEMAIL helps you manage and monitor email content before sending. It ensures compliance with your organization's policies by checking the subject and body before sending an email.

Features:

  • Prevents sending emails with empty subjects or bodies.
  • Ensures compliance with organizational email policies.
  • Works seamlessly within Microsoft Outlook.
